How Updraft Works

Propose & Fund Ideas

Anyone can suggest ideas for the public good. Funders support the most promising ideas and earn rewards for backing them early.

Draft Solutions

Builders draft solutions and raise funds to implement them, competing or collaborating to deliver real-world impact.

Organizations Guide

Organizations seed topics, offer bounties, and reward contributors to direct community effort where it's needed most.
